Breanna Allen

Graphic Design

Graphic design is something that is used every single day by people around the world. What inspired me to pursue graphic design was to be able to make an impact in this digital realm. My personal style includes lots of bright colors and playful designs. These designs are where my creativity gets to shine through, although I like to collaborate to create new styles and ideas. Ultimately, I’d love to work with clients and companies to help bring their ideas to life.

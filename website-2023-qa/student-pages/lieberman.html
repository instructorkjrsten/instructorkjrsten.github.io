Sarah Lieberman

Web Design & Front End Development

As an accessibility-focused designer, my designs are not only beautiful, but inclusive. I am a staunch believer that excellent design should be understood by all people, regardless of their abilities. Accessibility should be a top priority from the very start of the design process. My aesthetic is clean and uncomplicated, with an emphasis on strong contrast, typography, and intuitive navigation. I am dedicated to promoting accessibility and inclusion and am always learning new techniques to increase usability. Design should be a force for good, and accessibility is a critical to that goal.

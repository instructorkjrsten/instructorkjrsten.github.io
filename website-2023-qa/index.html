Welcome to the Minneapolis College Class of 2023
Graphic Design and Web Design Virtual Show!

This collection showcases this year’s 42 graduating designers’ talent as we celebrate each other’s accomplishments and transition into the next phase of our careers. In this show you will see the unique styles and voices we’ve developed throughout a global pandemic and times of local unrest. Our faces and work reflect the diverse identities and backgrounds that inform us as individual designers and comprise our community- as well as the adaptability required to be successful in both asynchronous programs and an increasingly remote industry.
We are excited to share how much we’ve learned and grown throughout our time at Minneapolis College and hope you enjoy the various creative approaches and styles presented in this exhibition.  
Please also join us for our virtual reception on May 2nd at 6 p.m. and again on May 3rd at 1 p.m. Where you will be able to meet the talented designers and inquire about their individual creative processes during the Q & A section of the presentation.

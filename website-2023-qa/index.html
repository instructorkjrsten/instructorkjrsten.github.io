the show

Welcome to the Minneapolis College Class of 2023 Graphic Design and Web Design programs’ Virtual Reception!

This collection showcases this year’s 42 graduating designers’ portfolios and celebrates our accomplishments. In this show you will see the unique styles and voices we’ve developed throughout a global pandemic and times of local unrest. Our work also displays the diverse identities and backgrounds that inform us as individual designers and comprise our community.

Our class is excited to share how much we’ve learned and grown throughout our time at Minneapolis College and we hope you enjoy the various creative approaches and styles presented in this exhibition.  

Please join us for our virtual reception May 2nd at 6 p.m. and again on May 3rd at 1 p.m.
During the Q & A section of the presentation, you will be able to meet the talented designers and dive deeper into their work.

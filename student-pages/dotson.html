Laura Dotson

Graphic Design

With an instinctive sense of composition and a love of color, I am passionate about good design. My work is influenced by the past and driven by curiosity. When I’m immersed in a design project I feel deeply focused and work hard to bring my designs to the highest standard. The graphic design program has allowed me to channel my skills into effective and visually pleasing designs. My listening skills and attention to detail complement my design sensibilities and aid me in the creative process. My personal style is clean and colorful with an enthusiasm for interesting type.
